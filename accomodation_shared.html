Shared Room partly with sea view

If you are a kitesurf-traveller who is searching for a more economic option we are happy to provide our neat and spacious dorm rooms to share with other kitesurfers. We have newly fitted-out a spacious 6-bed dorm with garden view and one sea side 8-bed dorm, right in front of the lagoon with an amazing sea view. Each dormitory offers a private bathroom with shower, strong handmade bunk beds with comfy mattresses, strong fans and and a fresh breeze of the sea. This is the perfect place to meet other kite-mates if you started your journey by yourself!

Our homemade breakfast buffet offers international and certainly Brazilian delights. Our guests appreciate our fresh variety of plant-based options to create an individual breakfast with different flavours.

Get in touch with us if you’d like to book a bed in one of our dorms!

* Prices are depending on the category of the room and the season.

Room capacity: 6 or 8 person

Rate is including:

- Natural and healthy breakfast buffet*
- Bed linen and comfy pillow
- Strong fans in the room
- Free Wifi
- Spacious deposit lockers in the rooms
- Bath room inside of the shared room
- Shared kite storage on the kite spot
- A beach boy who is helping with starting and landing your kite

- Room rates inclusive of tax - ​

Rate applies to 1 guest/per night and is depending on low/high season.

About

Situated in Itarema, Coco-Knots Kite Lodge features a garden and free WiFi. A buffet breakfast and vegetarian options are available each morning at this bed and breakfast. At Coco-Knots Kite Lodge you will find a restaurant serving international cuisine.
