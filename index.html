Our Concept

Take a break, settle down and kite-surf whenever the mood takes you.

Coco-Knots Kite Lodge is a laid-back guesthouse surrounded by palm trees, in front of our famous kitesurf-lagoon. The wind is blowing every day and it's a perfect spot for kitesurf-beginners and advanced riders.

Our Guesthouse is a combination of Kite-Pousada & Backpacker-Kite-Hostel. We offer either spacious private rooms with an amazing ocean view or cozy shared bed rooms for a fair travel budget. Both options include our fresh served breakfast buffet with an international diversity of tropical Brazilian selections and typical European varieties.

Our concept is to provide a sociable place where you have the chance to meet other kitesurfers, to hang out and exchange travel tips, relax in a comfortable kite surf environment and enjoy a healthy and peaceful life style.
