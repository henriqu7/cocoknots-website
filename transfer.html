Transfer


The closest airport to Ilha do Guajirú is in Fortaleza which is approx. 230 km south and will take you about 2,30 hours by taxi.
The new international airport in Cruz which is approx. 30 min. away from Ilha do Guajiru will be opening soon and offers international flights.

​ To get to us from the airport Fortaleza you have the choice between the bus, a rental car or a taxi transfer.

​ From Fortaleza by rental car:
The road from the airport Fortaleza to Ilha do Guajirú is almost new and the distance is approx. 230 km. Just follow the CE085 and pass Pecem, Taiba, Paracuru, Lagoinha, Flexeiras till Itarema. In Itarema just follow the beach signs ("Praia" = Beach). The daily rate for a economy car is approx. 35 EUR. We can organize the rental car for you which you can take over at the rental car office directly at the airport.

​ From Fortaleza by bus:
The most economical way the get to Ilha do Guajirú is by the bus company Fretcar. It's a big bus with comfy seats and aircon. The bus transports kite-luggage for free. It's leaving from the airport about 5 times a day to Jijoca (Jericoacoara) and is passing Itarema. The bus will stop at Itarema which is 6 km away from Ilha do Guajiru. At the bus station you will find a taxi which can take you to Ilha do Guajiru.
Recommendation: Better take warm clothes as the bus can be freezing cold sometimes.

From Fortaleza by privat transfer or taxi:
The most comfortable way is a privat transfer or a taxi. Following prices are per car for up to 4 people. The cheaper option is a normal car, in which the kite luggage is transported on the roof. The higher price is for a 4x4 pickup Hilux. We also arrange trips for more people.

​Please feel free to book your private transfer with us.

Transfer from Cumbuco to Ilha do Guajiru
In high season there is also once a day a bus connection between Cumbuco and Jericoacoara. The bus will stop in Itarema from where you easily can get to Ilha do Guajiru.
For the actual time table please send us an email.
You can book this transfer through us with at least one day noitice in advance.
