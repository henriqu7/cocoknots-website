Our Concept

Take a break, settle down and kite-surf whenever the mood takes you.

Ilha do Guajiru is a beautiful, remote and safe kite surf-beach in one of the windiest kite spots in Brazil. An excellent location for independent kite-surfers who like an easy access to their kite surf spot. Here you can kitesurf whenever the mood takes you within a sociable environment and a beautiful place to calm down, chill out and get in contact with other kitesurf-lovers from all over the world.

Come and enjoy our stunning sea view with outdoor seating and hammocks where you can relax under palm trees with a fresh cold coconut in your hand while listening to the wind whistling above.

Coco-Knots Kite Lodge is a laid-back guesthouse surrounded by palm trees, in front of our famous kitesurf-lagoon. The wind is blowing every day and it's a perfect spot for kitesurf-beginners and advanced riders.

Our Guesthouse is a combination of Kite-Pousada & Backpacker-Kite-Hostel. We offer either spacious private rooms with an amazing ocean view or cozy shared bed rooms for a fair travel budget. Both options include our fresh served breakfast buffet with an international diversity of tropical Brazilian selections and typical European varieties.
We are providing a kite storage, a gras area to launch, land and park your kite, a restaurant & beach bar, a chilled lounge area, certainly free WiFi throughout the entire area, guitar for our music lovers and of course gratis free-style kite surf entertainment in front of our lagoon.

We are more than just a place to sleep:
Our concept is to provide a sociable place where you have the chance to meet other kitesurfers, to hang out and exchange travel tips, relax in a comfortable kite surf environment and enjoy a healthy and peaceful life style.

If this is something you are looking for please get in touch with us or feel free to pass by and check out the newest Kite Lodge in town - Coco-Knots Kite Lodge.



About

Situated in Itarema, Coco-Knots Kite Lodge features a garden and free WiFi. A buffet breakfast and vegetarian options are available each morning at this bed and breakfast. At Coco-Knots Kite Lodge you will find a restaurant serving international cuisine.
