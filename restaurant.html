Restaurant & Bar

We are focusing on a healthy lifestyle and enjoy to offer our guests a natural wholesome cuisine in our restaurant and fresh drinks in our beach bar.
Our new restaurant provides a tasty vegetarian cuisine during day time and various selections of vegan meals and desserts during night time. Certainly fruity Caipirinhas and cold beer is part of the Brazilian life style and should never been missing in Brazil! Our different flavours of Caipirinhas or Caipiroskas are very liked by our guests as much as the Brazilian cold beer or our home-made lemonades, fresh juices & smoothies. For the tropical beach feeling we serve natural cold coconut-water.

If you are our guest but prefer another kind of cuisine please feel free to search for other options at our neighbour's in Ilha do Guajiru who also have a big variety of culinary delights:

  • The pousada behind us in the second line is serving all kind of different stone oven cuisine.
  • The barraca behind our pousada serves fried seafood in the basic typical Brazilian way of life.
  • The huge open bar and restaurant next door is offering every day different fusion cuisine.
  • The beautiful pousada at the end of our small main street is offering pizza, meat-BBQ and also fresh ice cream.
  • The grilled fish platter at "Portal do Barra" is a tasty option for a fair budget.
  • For a proper Italian cuisine please try the Italian restaurant of pousada "Casa da Suica" with their original fire-stone-oven.
  • And last but not least you can try Eric's fish BBQ (happening once a while) in the natural pousada "Kite Safari" which is about 1 km further south from here. It's possible to walk or to kite upwind to the pousada Kite Safari and come back by a short downwinder.

About

Situated in Itarema, Coco-Knots Kite Lodge features a garden and free WiFi. A buffet breakfast and vegetarian options are available each morning at this bed and breakfast. At Coco-Knots Kite Lodge you will find a restaurant serving international cuisine.
